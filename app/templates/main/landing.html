Evidence-Based Platform Features

Built on Design Science Research, Protection Motivation Theory, and Bloom's Taxonomy

Adaptive Rule Engine

Automatically assigns your difficulty level based on pre-assessment results. Upgrades or downgrades content in real time based on your performance streak.

7 Learning Modules

Structured modules covering Phishing, Social Engineering, Password Security, Safe Browsing, Pretexting, Data Handling, and Incident Reporting.

Gamification Engine

Earn XP, level up, unlock badges, and compete on the leaderboard. Gamification increases engagement and motivation to complete training.

Realistic Scenarios

Practice with simulated phishing emails, vishing calls, malicious websites, and USB baiting scenarios with immediate expert feedback.

Progress Analytics

Visual dashboards track your domain scores, module completion, XP earned, and compare your pre- vs post-assessment performance.

PMT-Aligned Design

Content designed using Protection Motivation Theory to address both threat appraisal and coping appraisal for behaviour change.

About This Research

GTDF is a prototype developed as part of an MSc dissertation titled "Designing a Personalised Gamified Cybersecurity Awareness Training Platform using Survey-Driven Evidence." The platform implements the Gamified Tailored Development Framework (GTDF) using Design Science Research Methodology (DSRM), grounded in empirical survey data from 51 participants (n=51).
